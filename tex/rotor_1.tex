Neglecting blade flapping motions occuring more frequent than once per revolution flapping angle can be expressed as:

\begin{equation}
\beta \left( \Psi \right)
=
\beta_0 + \beta_{1c} \cos \Psi + \beta_{1s} \sin \Psi 
\end{equation}
